## 📱 System Requirements

### ✅ Minimum Requirements
- **Android Version**: Android 10 or higher (Required)
- **RAM**: 4GB minimum (6GB+ recommended)
- **Storage**: 100MB free space for app
- **Camera**: Any camera with recording capability
- **Microphone**: Built-in or external

### 🔥 Recommended Specifications
- **Android Version**: Android 12+
- **RAM**: 8GB or higher
- **Processor**: Snapdragon 888 or equivalent
- **Storage**: 256GB+ for high-bitrate recording
- **Network**: 10+ Mbps upload speed for streaming

### 📊 Performance Targets
| Device Type | Max FPS | Max Resolution | Recommended Bitrate |
|---|---|---|---|
| Mid-Range (4-6GB RAM) | 30 FPS | 720p | 2-3 Mbps |
| High-End (8GB RAM) | 60 FPS | 1080p | 4-5 Mbps |
| Flagship (12GB+ RAM) | 60 FPS | 4K | 8-12 Mbps |

## 📥 Download & Installation

### Step 1️⃣: Download the APK

1. Visit **[livelens.live](https://www.livelens.live/)**
2. Click on **"Download APK"** button
3. Wait for the file to download (usually 50-100MB)

### Step 2️⃣: Enable Unknown Sources

1. Go to **Settings** → **Security** (or **Apps & Notifications**)
2. Look for **"Unknown Sources"** or **"Install Unknown Apps"**
3. Find your file manager or browser app
4. Toggle **ON** to allow installations from unknown sources

> **Note**: On Android 12+, you can grant permission per app instead of globally

### Step 3️⃣: Install the APK

1. Open your **Downloads** folder or file manager
2. Find the **LiveLens APK** file
3. Tap on it to start installation
4. Tap **"Install"** when prompted
5. Wait for installation to complete

### Step 4️⃣: Grant Permissions

1. Open **LiveLens** from your app drawer
2. Grant the following permissions when prompted:
   - ✅ **Camera** - For recording video
   - ✅ **Microphone** - For capturing audio
   - ✅ **Storage** - For saving scenes and recordings
   - ✅ **Overlay** (if asked) - For floating controls

> **Tip**: You can grant these later in Settings → Apps → LiveLens → Permissions

### Step 5️⃣: Launch LiveLens

- Tap on **LiveLens** icon to open the app
- You're ready to go! 🎉

## ⚙️ Initial Setup (2 minutes)

### 1. Welcome Screen
When you open LiveLens for the first time, you'll see the **Welcome Setup**:

### 2. Select Canvas Size

Choose your default canvas size:
- **16:9** - For desktop/laptop displays (YouTube, Twitch)
- **9:16** - For vertical/mobile streams (Instagram, TikTok)

> **You can change this later in Settings**

### 3. Configure Audio Input

Select your audio sources:
- **Microphone** - Your device's built-in mic
- **System Audio** - Audio from other apps
- **Both** - Microphone + System audio (recommended)

> **Audio will be automatically handled**, but you can customize later

### 4. Notification Settings

Allow or disable notifications:
- Live notifications (optional)
- Stream status alerts (recommended)

### 5. Done! ✨

Your LiveLens is now configured and ready to use!

## 🎬 Create Your First Scene

### Step 1: Open Scene Editor

1. From the **Home** screen, tap **"+"** button
2. Select **"Create New Scene"**
3. Give your scene a name (e.g., "My First Stream")

### Step 2: Add a Camera Source

1. In the scene editor, tap **"Add Source"**
2. Select **"Camera"**
3. Choose your camera (Front or Back)
4. Tap **"Add"**
5. The camera feed will appear on the canvas

### Step 3: Resize & Position

1. **Tap and drag** the camera feed to move it
2. **Pinch and zoom** to resize
3. **Rotate** using the rotation handle (if available)

> **Tip**: Use alignment guides to center your content

### Step 4: Add a Text Overlay

1. Tap **"Add Source"** again
2. Select **"Text"**
3. Enter your text (e.g., "My Channel" or your name)
4. Tap **"Add"**

### Step 5: Style Your Text

1. Select the text layer
2. Tap the **brush icon** to customize:
   - Font size
   - Color
   - Font family
   - Transparency
   - Shadow/effects

### Step 6: Preview Your Scene

1. Tap **"Preview"** to see how it looks
2. The preview shows exactly what will be streamed
3. Make adjustments as needed

### Step 7: Save Your Scene

1. Tap **"Save"** or use the back button
2. Your scene is now saved and ready!

## 🌐 Configure Streaming

### Step 1: Go to Streaming Settings

1. From the **Home** screen, tap **⚙️ Settings**
2. Scroll down and select **"Streaming"**

### Step 2: Enter RTMP URL

1. Get your **RTMP URL** from your platform:
   - **Twitch**: Settings → Stream → Primary Stream Key
   - **YouTube**: Go Live → Stream URL + Stream Key
   - **Facebook**: Videos → Go Live → Stream Key
   - **Custom RTMP**: From your server

2. Paste the URL in **RTMP URL** field

### Step 3: Configure Stream Settings

| Setting | Recommended | Details |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| **Resolution** | 1080p (if supported) | Higher = better quality but more resources |
| **FPS** | 60 (if device supports) | 30 FPS is safer for older devices |
| **Bitrate** | 4-5 Mbps | Higher = better quality, needs faster internet |
| **Keyframe Interval** | 2 seconds | Leave default unless you know what you're doing |
| **Encoder** | H.264 | H.265 is better but less compatible |

### Step 4: Test Your Connection

1. Tap **"Test Connection"**
2. LiveLens will check your internet speed
3. If successful, you're ready to stream!

> **Note**: Make sure you have 10+ Mbps upload speed

### Step 5: Audio Configuration

1. Go to **"Audio Settings"**
2. Enable:
   - ✅ **Microphone** - Your voice
   - ✅ **System Audio** - Game/music audio
3. Adjust levels as needed

## 📡 Start Streaming

### Pre-Stream Checklist

- [ ] Internet connection is stable (10+ Mbps upload)
- [ ] Camera is positioned correctly
- [ ] Microphone is not blocked
- [ ] Lighting is good
- [ ] Your scene is ready
- [ ] RTMP settings are configured

### Live Stream Steps

1. **Select your scene** from the home screen
2. Tap **"Go Live"** button (big red button)
3. LiveLens will:
   - Connect to your streaming platform
   - Start encoding your scene
   - Begin transmitting to the platform

4. **Watch your preview** on screen
5. **Monitor audio levels** with the audio meters
6. **Adjust sources in real-time** if needed

### During Stream

- 🎤 Tap sources to hide/show them
- 📊 Watch bitrate/FPS at the top
- 🔊 Monitor audio levels
- 🎚️ Adjust settings in real-time
- 🎬 Switch between overlays if needed

### Stop Streaming

1. Tap the **"Stop"** button (red stop icon)
2. LiveLens will end the broadcast
3. You can review the session stats

## 🎮 Tips & Tricks

### ✨ Optimize Quality

1. **Good Lighting** - Better lighting = better quality
2. **Stable Internet** - Wired connection is better than WiFi
3. **Close Other Apps** - Free up RAM for smoother streaming
4. **Lower Resolution if Lagging** - 720p at 60 FPS is better than 1080p at 30 FPS

### 🎨 Scene Management

- **Duplicate Scenes** - Copy a scene to create variations
- **Import/Export** - Save scenes as JSON backups
- **Scene Templates** - Use pre-made templates

### 🔊 Audio Tips

- **Test Mic First** - Check audio levels before going live
- **Use External Mic** - Better quality than built-in
- **Noise Suppression** - Enable if background noise is high
- **Monitor in Headphones** - Wear headphones to check quality

### 📱 Device Tips

- **Landscape Mode** - More stable for streaming
- **Airplane Mode** - Close WiFi only, keep mobile data for calls
- **Avoid Notifications** - Turn off notifications during stream
- **Charge Device** - Keep battery above 50% during stream

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### ❌ Installation Issues

**Problem**: "App not installed" error
- **Solution**: 
  - Clear your Downloads cache
  - Check storage space (100MB+ needed)
  - Try re-downloading the APK

**Problem**: "Unknown app" permission denied
- **Solution**:
  - Go to Settings → Security
  - Enable "Unknown Sources"
  - Use file manager to install, not browser

### ❌ Permission Issues

**Problem**: Camera/Microphone not working
- **Solution**:
  - Go to Settings → Apps → LiveLens → Permissions
  - Enable Camera and Microphone
  - Restart LiveLens

**Problem**: Can't record/save files
- **Solution**:
  - Grant Storage permission
  - Check if phone storage is full
  - Free up at least 1GB space

### ❌ Streaming Issues

**Problem**: "Connection failed" error
- **Solution**:
  - Check internet speed (10+ Mbps needed)
  - Try using WiFi instead of mobile data
  - Verify RTMP URL is correct
  - Restart the app

**Problem**: Stream keeps disconnecting
- **Solution**:
  - Lower bitrate by 1-2 Mbps
  - Reduce resolution or FPS
  - Move closer to WiFi router
  - Close other apps using internet

**Problem**: Audio not working in stream
- **Solution**:
  - Check microphone permission
  - Tap microphone icon to enable
  - Test audio levels before streaming
  - Use external microphone if built-in fails

### ❌ Performance Issues

**Problem**: FPS dropping/lag
- **Solution**:
  - Close background apps
  - Lower resolution or FPS
  - Remove unnecessary sources
  - Restart device if memory is full

**Problem**: App crashes during stream
- **Solution**:
  - Update LiveLens to latest version
  - Reduce source count in scene
  - Lower bitrate
  - Use a device with more RAM

### ❌ Scene Issues

**Problem**: Sources not appearing
- **Solution**:
  - Check if source is hidden (tap to toggle)
  - Verify permissions are granted
  - Try re-adding the source

**Problem**: Scene won't save
- **Solution**:
  - Check storage space
  - Restart app
  - Try importing/exporting as backup
