# LiveLens Monetization

## Our Philosophy

LiveLens is built with a simple goal:

**Give creators powerful mobile streaming tools without limiting them through ads, subscriptions, or watermarks.**

We believe that creators should focus on creating content, not removing artificial restrictions.

---

## Monetization Model

LiveLens is primarily supported through **sponsorships**.

Instead of relying on advertisements or locking features behind subscriptions, we aim to work with sponsors who help support the continued development of the project.

This allows LiveLens to remain:

- ✅ Free to use
- ✅ No advertisements
- ✅ No subscriptions
- ✅ No watermarks
- ✅ Full access to core streaming features

---

## Community Support

Using LiveLens will always be free.

However, if you enjoy the project and would like to support its development, you are welcome to make an optional donation.

Community support is never required and will never unlock exclusive streaming features.

Your support simply helps keep development moving forward.

---

## Why Sponsorships?

Many streaming applications rely on advertisements, subscriptions, or feature restrictions to generate revenue.

LiveLens follows a different approach.

By prioritizing sponsorships, we can continue developing the app while keeping the experience clean and accessible for everyone.

Our goal is to ensure that creators can stream without:

- Interruptive advertisements
- Subscription paywalls
- Watermarks on their content
- Locked core functionality

---

## Our Commitment

Our long-term vision is to keep LiveLens open and accessible while building a sustainable project.

We will continue exploring funding methods that respect creators and preserve the core experience.

Whenever possible, LiveLens will remain:

- Free
- Ad-free
- Watermark-free
- Subscription-free for core features

---

## Thank You

Whether you use LiveLens, report bugs, suggest new ideas, contribute code, or choose to support the project financially, every contribution helps make LiveLens better for everyone.

Thank you for being part of the journey.
